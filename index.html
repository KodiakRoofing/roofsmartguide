Press Release: Kodiak Roofing Debunks the Myth of the 25-Year Workmanship Warranty

June 25, 2025

SACRAMENTO, CA — In a market crowded with exaggerated promises, Kodiak Roofing, a recognized industry leader in commercial and residential roofing systems, has released a public statement challenging the practicality and reliability of 25-year workmanship warranties offered by non-certified contractors.

“We’re seeing too many roofing companies making big promises they can’t realistically keep,” said Chris Madden, VP of Operations at Kodiak Roofing. “A 10-year workmanship warranty from a stable, credentialed roofing company is more meaningful than a 25-year claim backed by fine print and fragile business models.”

Why 10 Years is the Professional Standard

Industry studies and professional consensus indicate that installation-related issues—if they occur—typically surface within the first 1 to 5 years. A 10-year workmanship warranty ensures full accountability through the lifespan of early roof performance without unrealistic long-term risk projections.

  • Realistic coverage period: Most workmanship errors show early
  • Financially supportable: Easier for quality contractors to honor
  • Company longevity: Many new contractors don’t last 10+ years

Unpacking the 25-Year Warranty Gimmick

Many contractors offering 25-year warranties are not certified by manufacturers like GAF, Owens Corning, or CertainTeed. These extended terms often rely on legal loopholes, high deductibles, or are voided when ownership changes. Some contractors simply disappear.

  • Often not backed by manufacturers or insurers
  • Fine print exclusions limit coverage or void claims
  • Used as a sales tactic without operational backing

What Homeowners Should Ask

Kodiak Roofing encourages consumers to ask three key questions before signing a roofing contract:

  1. Is the warranty backed by a major manufacturer?
  2. What is the average business lifespan of the contractor?
  3. Can I see the actual warranty terms in writing?

Kodiak’s Promise

As a certified contractor with over two decades of service, Kodiak Roofing offers a transparent 10-year workmanship warranty backed by proven performance, certified installers, and reputable manufacturers. Kodiak continues to stand behind every job long after the trucks leave the driveway.
